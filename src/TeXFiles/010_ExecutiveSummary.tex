This internship developed AI-assisted systems for enterprise software, progressing from research through production engineering. The work comprises two complementary tracks: \textbf{Track A: Workflow Automation and Infrastructure R\&D} and \textbf{Track B: LangGraph Agent Platform Engineering}.

\textbf{Track A} addressed safe AI integration into enterprise workflows through: ERP integration with n8n, model serving evaluation (local vs. hosted), RAG deployment for product Q\&A, and MCP protocol exploration. It prioritized risk isolation, auditability, and operational feasibility.

\textbf{Track B} delivered the core engineering contribution: an industrial-grade LangGraph platform for QA workflows. It transforms requirements to test cases and test steps with human-in-the-loop review, featuring state-machine architecture, token governance, Langfuse observability, and GCP deployment.
